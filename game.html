How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move.
  • Eat the glowing food to grow and increase speed.
  • Avoid walls, your tail, and the red obstacles.
  • Every few points make the game faster.

Game Features

Obstacles are placed in the arena, the speed ramps up as you score, and your highest score is saved automatically.
